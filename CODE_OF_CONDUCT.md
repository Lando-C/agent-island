# Code of Conduct

Agent Island follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex
characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on code, behavior, and product outcomes.
- Assume good intent while still being precise about risks and bugs.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory language.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block contributors who violate
this code. Security-sensitive reports should use `SECURITY.md`.

For the full Contributor Covenant text, see:

https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
